Donuts

Desciption

Doughnuts, or donuts, are a classic sweet goodie that have long been enjoyed in some version in nearly all corners of the world. Thick, rich, sweet dough is formed into a circle – with or without a hole – and either baked or fried to fluffy, tender perfection.

If you're afraid of frying them, invest in a thermomenter. Knowing the oil temperature makes a huge difference.

Ingredients

For the donut:

  • 2 tablespoon butter (room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup Whole milk (warm)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Active Dry yeast
  • Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups All purpose flour
  • Water (as required)
  • Oil

For the chocolate glaze:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoon mill

Steps

  1. Activate yeast by adding milk and sugar

    2. In a large mixing bowl, take 1/2 cup warm milk or water. Add 1 tsp of sugar to that. Also add 1 tsp of dry yeast and give a quick mix. Allow it to rest till it turns frothy

  2. Add flour, baking powder, butter and salt.

    3. Add them to the bowl and comine and crumble well.

  3. Combine and knead to smooth dough adding water as required.

    4. Knead till the dough is soft, smooth, elastic and also bounces back when pressed.

  4. Grease, cover and rest for 1-2 hours.

    5. Tuck the dough and grease with oil. Cover the bowl tightly with cling wrap or plastic wrap and allow to rest and rise in a warm place

  5. Punch the dough and roll it slightly thick
  6. With the help of donut cutter, cut round

    7. Brush the donuts with oil to prevent from drying out. Allow it to rest for 2 hours.

  7. Deep fry in medium hot oil to golden brown.
  8. Make the chocolate glaze

    9. Mix all ingredients and stir well till the mixture formed is smooth and creamy.

  9. Dip the doughnuts and serve chocolate donuts sprinkled with whatever.
