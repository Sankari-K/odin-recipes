Cheesecake

Description

This is a classic cheesecake recipe with graham cracker crust. You will need cream cheese to make this cheesecake. Also you will need a spring pan or a pan with a detachable base, so that you can easily remove the cheesecake without breaking it.

Ingredients

For the graham cracker crust:

  • 10-12 Graham crackers
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Salt (as required)

For the cheesecake filling:

  • 3 cups cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preparing biscuit crust for cheesecake

    2. Take 10-12 crackers, break them and add into a blender. Grind it into a fine powder and transfer it to a mixing bowl. Mix butter with this and press this biscuit + butter mixture to a small glass or bowl. Keep this in the fridge so that the biscuit layer sets.

  2. Making the cheesecake filling

    3. Take the cream cheese, sugar, and the vanilla extract and mix them together. With an electric beater, beat this mixture. Whip till smooth. Now add 1/2 cup cream. Again beat the mixture till the cream is blended very well and you see a homogenous, creamy, thick smooth mixture.

  3. Baking the cheesecake

    4. Now pour the whipped cream cheese layer in the pan. With a spatula, even the top.

    Preheat oven at 180Â° celsius for 15 minutes. Keep the pan in a preheated oven and bake for 30-40 minutes.

    To check doneness, the sides will look firm, risen and the center of the cheesecake will be wobbly.

  4. Cooling the cheesecake

    5. Let the cheesecake cool at room temperature. Then cover with a foil or an air tight lid and place the pans in the fridge for 4 hours or more. You can also keep it overnight.
